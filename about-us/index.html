About Stain & Seal Pros — Springfield's Wood Care Specialists

Meet the team behind the stain.

Team Photo

How It All Started

Stain & Seal Pros was born from a simple frustration: too many Springfield homeowners were getting mediocre wood care from companies that treated it as an afterthought. Ty Mackey and Scott Pitts saw a gap — no one in the Springfield area was specializing in outdoor wood care with the kind of precision and pride the work deserved.

So they built a company around doing one thing and doing it right. Every fence, every deck, every log home gets the same attention to detail — because that's what your investment deserves. Today, Stain & Seal Pros serves Springfield and the surrounding Southwest Missouri area with a growing list of happy homeowners and a 5-star reputation.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

Protect and beautify every outdoor wood surface we touch — with expert care, premium products, and genuine service that makes homeowners smile.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted wood care company in Southwest Missouri — known for quality work, honest pricing, and customers who recommend us to everyone they know.

Our Values

Quality over shortcuts. Honesty over sales tactics. Community over corporate. Every decision we make starts with: "Would we do this on our own home?"

Meet the Founders

TM

Ty Mackey

Co-Founder & Field Operations Lead

Ty leads every project from the ground up. With years of hands-on wood care experience and a background in construction and project management, he brings a craftsman's eye to every fence, deck, and log home the team touches. Ty is the guy on-site making sure the prep is right, the stain is applied correctly, and the finished result meets his personal standard — which is high. A Springfield native, Ty built Stain & Seal Pros on the belief that local homeowners deserve better than what the market was giving them.

SP

Scott Pitts

Co-Founder & Customer Experience Lead

Scott runs the business side — estimates, scheduling, customer communication, and making sure every homeowner feels taken care of from the first call to the final walkthrough. With a background in sales and customer service, Scott built the company's reputation on a simple idea: be the company you'd actually want to hire. He handles every quote personally, answers his phone, and follows up on every job. Springfield born and raised, Scott takes pride in building a business his community can count on.

Locally Owned. Springfield Proud.

We're not a national chain — we're your neighbors. Ty and Scott live in Springfield, and our crew works right here in the community we serve. When you hire Stain & Seal Pros, you're supporting a local business that cares about this city as much as you do.
