Wood Staining & Sealing in Springfield, MO

Springfield is our home base and where Stain & Seal Pros was founded by Ty Mackey and Scott Pitts. As the heart of the Ozarks region, Springfield's climate — with hot summers, freezing winters, and plenty of rain — puts serious demands on exposed wood. That's exactly why we started this business: to give Springfield homeowners a reliable, detail-focused staining and sealing crew they can count on year after year.

Springfield's Trusted Wood Staining Experts

From the established neighborhoods of Rountree and Phelps Grove to the newer developments in south Springfield and Galloway Village, we've stained and sealed hundreds of fences, decks, pergolas, and log homes across the city. Springfield's mix of older homes with mature trees and new construction keeps our crew busy — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

We use commercial-grade stains and sealants from brands like TWP, Armstrong Clark, and Ready Seal. Every job starts with proper surface preparation — cleaning, sanding, and brightening as needed — because a great stain job is only as good as the prep work underneath it. No shortcuts, no spray-and-pray.
