Log Home Restoration & Staining in the Springfield, MO Area

Complete care for your Ozarks log home — from finish removal to staining, chinking, and beyond.

Log Home Restoration Photo – Ozarks, MO

Log homes are a part of the Ozarks. Whether it's a family cabin tucked into the hills or a full-time residence outside Springfield, these homes demand a different level of care than standard construction. The wood is exposed, the joints are vulnerable, and Missouri's climate doesn't go easy on any of it.

At Stain & Seal Pros, log home restoration is one of our core specialties. We handle everything from routine maintenance staining to full-scale restoration — including finish removal, chinking repair, borate treatment, rot repair, and interior finishing. Our approach is thorough, our products are professional-grade, and every project starts with a hands-on assessment.

Complete Log Home Services

Finish Removal

Strip old, failing stain or paint down to bare wood using media blasting, chemical stripping, or corn cob blasting — chosen based on your home's needs.

Crack & Gap Repair

Fill checks, cracks, and gaps in the log surface to prevent water infiltration and insect entry. We use flexible fillers that move with the wood.

Chinking

Apply or replace chinking between logs — the flexible sealant that keeps air, water, and pests out. Essential for energy efficiency and structural health.

Caulking

Seal around windows, doors, and trim joints where moisture loves to sneak in. Proper caulking prevents rot at the most vulnerable points of the structure.

Interior Finishing

Stain, seal, and protect interior log walls and ceilings. We enhance the natural beauty of the wood while protecting it from everyday wear and humidity.

Borate Treatment

Professional borate wood preservative that protects against beetles, carpenter ants, termites, and fungal decay. Penetrates deep for long-lasting defense.

Staining & Sealing

Apply premium log home stain and UV-resistant sealant. We match your preferred color and use products formulated specifically for log home applications.

Rot Repair

Remove and replace rotted log sections, window sills, and structural components. We match the wood species and finish so repairs blend seamlessly.

Pest Control Prep

Identify and address insect damage, then treat and seal to prevent future infestations. Works alongside borate treatment for comprehensive protection.

Annual Maintenance

Scheduled inspections and touch-up services to catch issues early. Keeps your log home in peak condition year after year without major restoration costs.

Log Home FAQs

Most log homes in the Ozarks need restaining every 3–5 years, depending on sun exposure, elevation, and the quality of the previous finish. South and west-facing walls break down faster. We recommend an annual visual inspection and can set up a maintenance schedule so nothing gets missed.

Chinking is the flexible sealant applied between logs to fill gaps and prevent air, water, and insects from getting inside. If your logs have shifted over time or you see daylight between them, chinking needs to be inspected and possibly replaced. It's one of the most important parts of log home maintenance — without it, moisture gets trapped and causes rot from the inside out.

Borate treatment is a wood preservative applied to log surfaces that protects against wood-boring insects like beetles, carpenter ants, and termites, as well as fungal decay and rot. It penetrates into the wood and provides long-lasting protection. For log homes in the Ozarks, where insect pressure is real, borate treatment is one of the smartest preventive steps you can take.

Maintenance means cleaning, light prep, and reapplying stain — typically for homes that have been kept up. Restoration is for homes where the finish has failed, wood is graying, there's rot or insect damage, or the chinking has deteriorated. If you're not sure, book a free Discovery Consultation and we'll assess the condition on-site.

The goal is to get back to clean, bare wood without damaging the log surface. Once stripped, we treat and restain for a fresh, long-lasting finish.
