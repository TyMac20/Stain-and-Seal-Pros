Pergola & Outdoor Structure Staining in Springfield, MO

Professional staining and sealing for every outdoor wood structure in your backyard.

Pergola Staining Photo – Springfield, MO

Pergolas, arbors, playsets, and outdoor structures are built to be enjoyed — but they're also fully exposed to everything Missouri throws at them. UV, rain, humidity, and freeze-thaw cycles will break down unprotected wood faster than most homeowners realize.

At Stain & Seal Pros, we stain and seal every type of outdoor wood structure. From a brand-new kit pergola to a weathered backyard playset, we apply the right prep and the right product so your structure looks great and lasts years longer.

New Structure

Just built or purchased a new pergola, playset, or arbor? We clean and apply the first coat of stain and sealant while the wood is fresh. For pressure-treated lumber, we test moisture to make sure it's ready.

Existing Structure

Fading, graying, or losing its color? We clean, brighten, and restain to bring back the original look and add years of protection. Maintenance staining keeps your structure in top shape.

Restoration

Heavily weathered, cracked, or neglected? We strip old finish, repair damage, and refinish from the ground up. Full restoration brings even the most tired structures back to life.

3

Weathered

Graying, rough texture. Needs cleaning, brightening, and fresh stain.

4

Damaged

Cracking, splitting, structural issues. Full restoration with repairs.
