Professional Wood Staining & Sealing Services in Springfield, MO

From fences to log homes — expert care for every outdoor wood surface.

Service Photo

At Stain & Seal Pros, we specialize in one thing: protecting and beautifying outdoor wood. Whether you have a brand-new cedar fence that needs pre-staining before installation, a weathered deck that needs restoration, or a log home that needs full maintenance — our Springfield team has the products, the process, and the experience to get it done right.

We serve Springfield, Ozark, Nixa, Republic, and the surrounding Southwest Missouri area. Every job is handled by our own crew — we never subcontract. Browse our services below or call us for a free, no-pressure estimate.

Our Services

Wood Fence Staining & Sealing

Enhance and protect your wood fence with expert staining and sealing. We restore beauty and extend the life of cedar, pine, and redwood fences across Springfield and beyond.

Wood Deck Staining & Sealing

Protect your deck from Missouri's weather, UV, and everyday wear with professional staining and sealing. Enjoy your outdoor living space with confidence and peace of mind.

Log Home Restoration

Complete care for your log and timber home — from finish removal and chinking to borate treatment and staining. Expert restoration for the Springfield and Ozarks area.

Pergolas & Outdoor Structures
