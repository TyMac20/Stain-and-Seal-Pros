Wood Fence Staining & Sealing in Springfield, MO

Expert staining and protection for cedar, pine, and redwood fences across Southwest Missouri.

Fence Staining Photo – Springfield, MO

Your fence is the first thing people see — and in Springfield's climate, it takes a beating. Summer heat, winter freeze-thaw, rain, and UV exposure all work against bare or poorly sealed wood. Whether your fence is brand new or showing its age, professional staining and sealing is the smartest investment you can make to keep it looking great and lasting longer.

At Stain & Seal Pros, fence staining is what we do best. We handle everything from pre-staining new boards before installation to full restoration of weathered, grayed-out fences. Our crew is local, our products are commercial-grade, and every job gets the same attention to detail — no shortcuts.

Protect Your Fence From Day One

Building a new fence? The best time to stain is before installation. Our pre-stain service coats every board on all four sides — top, bottom, front, and back — so moisture can't sneak in through the cuts and joints that are impossible to reach once the fence is up.

Pre-staining prevents warping, cracking, and premature rot. It's the kind of protection that pays for itself in years of fence life. We work directly with homeowners and fence contractors across Springfield, Ozark, and Nixa.

Already Installed? Restore & Maintain

Fence Restoration Photo

Got a fence that's turned gray, started splitting, or just lost its color? We bring it back. Our fence restoration process includes cleaning, brightening, minor repairs, and fresh stain application. We match colors to your preference and use products formulated for Missouri's climate.

Regular maintenance staining every 2–4 years keeps your fence looking sharp and prevents costly replacements. We'll assess the condition and tell you exactly what your fence needs — no upselling, no surprises.

What Stage Is Your Fence In?

Every fence is different. Here's how we approach each stage — from brand new to fully weathered.

1

Brand New (Pre-Install)

Boards haven't been installed yet. We pre-stain all four sides for maximum protection before they go up.

2

New & Unstained

Fence is up but never been stained. We clean, prep, and apply the first coat while the wood is still fresh.

3

Fading & Aging

Color is fading, maybe some graying. We clean, brighten, and restain to bring back the original look.

4

Weathered & Damaged

Gray, cracked, or warped boards. Full restoration including repairs, stripping, and restaining.

Fence Staining FAQs

Most residential fence staining projects in Springfield range from $1.50 to $4.00 per square foot depending on fence height, condition, and stain product. A typical 150-linear-foot privacy fence runs roughly $800–$1,800. We provide free on-site estimates so you get an exact price before any work begins.

In the Springfield area, a quality fence stain typically lasts 2–4 years depending on sun exposure, drainage, and the product used. South-facing sections take more UV and may need attention sooner. We use commercial-grade stains rated for Missouri's freeze-thaw cycles and summer humidity.

Cedar looks great in warm tones like Pecan, Natural Cedar, and Dark Walnut. Semi-transparent stains are the most popular because they let the wood grain show through while providing solid UV protection. We bring color samples to every estimate so you can see options on your actual wood.

We use a controlled low-pressure wash with a wood-safe cleaning solution — not the high-pressure blasting that damages wood fibers. Proper prep is the difference between a stain that lasts and one that peels.
