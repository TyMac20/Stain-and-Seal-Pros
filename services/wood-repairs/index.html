Wood Fence & Deck Repair in Springfield, MO

Rotted boards, cracked pickets, storm damage — we fix it all and stain it to match.

Wood Repair Photo – Springfield, MO

A few rotted boards don't mean you need a whole new fence. A cracked deck plank doesn't mean it's time to tear everything out. Most wood damage in the Springfield area is fixable — and when you catch it early, repairs save you thousands compared to full replacement.

At Stain & Seal Pros, we handle wood repairs as part of our full restoration process. We don't just slap in a new board and walk away — we fix the problem, prep the surface, and stain it to match. One visit. One crew. One result you'll be proud of.

What We Repair

Rotted Boards

Fence boards and deck planks weakened by moisture and rot — removed and replaced with matching lumber.

Cracked & Split Pickets

Cracked pickets compromise your fence's look and structure. We replace them individually so you don't pay for a full panel.

Damaged & Leaning Posts

Leaning or broken posts are a structural problem. We reset or replace posts and reinforce them properly.

Loose & Broken Rails

Loose horizontal rails weaken entire fence sections. We re-secure or replace rails to restore full structural integrity.

Warped Deck Boards

Cupped, bowed, or twisted deck boards are a tripping hazard and an eyesore. We pull and replace them cleanly.

Storm & Impact Damage

Springfield storms hit hard. We prioritize storm damage repairs and get your fence or deck back to solid shape fast.

Fix It & Protect It — In One Visit

Most repairs look best when followed by staining — so we bundle them. In one visit, we repair the damage, prep the surface, and apply a fresh stain and seal coat. You get a fully restored result without scheduling two separate appointments. It's the smartest way to handle wood damage.
