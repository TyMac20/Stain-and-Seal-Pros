How We Work — The Stain & Seal Pros Process

From your first call to the final walkthrough — here's exactly what to expect.

 The Pros Guarantee

We don't leave until it's right. Every job comes with our satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy, we come back and make it right. No runaround. No excuses.

1

Book Your Free Estimate

Call us at 417-496-4694 or fill out our online form. We respond same or next business day and schedule an on-site estimate at your convenience. No obligation — ever.

2

We Assess Your Project

Ty or Scott visits your property in person, evaluates the wood condition, moisture levels, and any existing finish — then recommends the right treatment plan for your specific project. No guesswork.

"Ty took the time to explain everything — what the wood needed, which stain would last longest, and exactly what it would cost. No surprises." — Sarah M., Springfield
3

Deep Clean & Prep

We use professional eco-safe cleaners to remove mold, mildew, UV gray, and old finish. Proper prep is what separates a stain job that lasts from one that peels. We never skip this step.

4

Premium Stain & Seal Application

We apply professional-grade oil-based stain that penetrates deep into the wood grain, locking out moisture and UV damage. We use only products we'd put on our own homes — because we live here too.

5

Final Walkthrough With You

Before we leave, we walk the entire job with you and make sure you're 100% satisfied. If anything needs attention, we handle it on the spot. This is where the Pros Guarantee kicks in.

"The walkthrough made us feel totally confident in the result. They pointed out every detail and made sure we were happy before they left." — Mike T., Ozark
6

Ongoing Maintenance Partnership

We'll remind you when it's time for your next maintenance coat — so your wood always looks its best without you having to track it. Most stain jobs last 2–4 years in Missouri's climate. We'll make sure yours goes the distance.

 Never Subcontracted — Ever

Every job is done by Ty, Scott, and our own crew — not a subcontractor. You know who's showing up. You know who to call. That's how we build trust in Springfield.
